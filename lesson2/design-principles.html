Contrast

IMDB

imdb.com

IMDB uses a black background as a base. This is then overlain by contrasting colors such as white text, yellow buttons and section headings, and light blue links. This improves the readability of the page allowing the user to more quickly navigate the site.

Repetition

Apple Inc.

apple.com

Apple uses repetition on their website. They primarily use a white background. Then section tiles alternating between black and the original white background. This also allows for easier navigation between topics.

Rule of Thirds

Amazon.com Inc.

amazon.com

Amazon uses the Rule of Thirds to make their shopping interface more appealing to users. Like many other shops, they divide the screen into a grid, showing different products, sections, and other useful links. This makes it easier for the user to more quickly scan the page.
