Site Name

North Ogden City Chamber of Commerce. This is the name by which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The North Ogden City Chamber of Commerce acts as a central hub for the leaders, businesses, citizens and visitors of the city. We promote business through member services, public policy, and community engagement. We pride ourselves on providing all with opportunity for growth and economic development through social contribution and participation.

Scenarios

  • How can I contact local leaders regarding public matters or concerns?
  • Where can I go to promote my businessand grow local connections?
  • What restaurants and shops are locally owned?
  • Where can I find a list of current activities for my family to enjoy?
